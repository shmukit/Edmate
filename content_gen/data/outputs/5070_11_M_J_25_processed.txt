Question 1Question and Options in Text Format

Four gases are listed.1 CH₄2 NH₃3 CO₂4 N₂

What is the order of their rate of diffusion at room temperature and pressure?

A. 2, 1, 4, 3B. 1, 2, 3, 4C. 3, 4, 2, 1D. 4, 1, 3, 2

Detailed Explanation of the Question and Right Answer

The rate of diffusion of a gas is inversely proportional to the square root of its relative molecular mass (Mᵣ). Lighter gases diffuse faster, while heavier gases diffuse slower.

To solve this, we must calculate the Mᵣ for each gas and order them from highest mass (slowest) to lowest mass (fastest).

Step 1: Calculate the Relative Molecular Mass (Mᵣ) for each gas using atomic masses (C = 12, H = 1, N = 14, O = 16).

Methane (CH₄):12 + (4 × 1) = 16

Ammonia (NH₃):14 + (3 × 1) = 17

Carbon dioxide (CO₂):12 + (2 × 16) = 44

Nitrogen (N₂):14 × 2 = 28

Step 2: Arrange the gases by mass in descending order (Heaviest to Lightest) to determine the diffusion rate (Slowest to Fastest).

Heaviest: CO₂ (Mᵣ 44) → Gas 3Second heaviest: N₂ (Mᵣ 28) → Gas 4Third heaviest: NH₃ (Mᵣ 17) → Gas 2Lightest: CH₄ (Mᵣ 16) → Gas 1

Step 3: Establish the final sequence.

Slowest (CO₂, 3) → (N₂, 4) → (NH₃, 2) → Fastest (CH₄, 1)

Final Correct Answer:The correct order is 3, 4, 2, 1, which corresponds to Option C.

Option Wise Explanation (Detailed)

Option A:This option suggests ammonia (2) is the slowest and carbon dioxide (3) is the fastest. This is incorrect because ammonia (Mᵣ 17) is much lighter than carbon dioxide (Mᵣ 44), so ammonia would diffuse faster, not slower.

Option B:This option suggests methane (1) is the slowest and nitrogen (4) is the fastest. This contradicts the principle of diffusion, as methane is the lightest gas (Mᵣ 16) and should be the fastest, not the slowest.

Option C:This option correctly lists the gases in order of decreasing molecular mass: CO₂ (44), N₂ (28), NH₃ (17), and CH₄ (16). This matches the physical law that heavier gases diffuse more slowly than lighter gases.

Option D:This option places nitrogen (4) as the slowest and ammonia (2) as the fastest. While nitrogen is heavier than ammonia, carbon dioxide (3) is heavier than nitrogen and should be the slowest.
