# QC Rubric: A/O-Level Content

**Pass Score: 8/10**

## 1. Structural Integrity (3 Points)
*   **1 pt**: Follows `PROCESS_GUIDE.md` structure exactly (Headings, Order).
*   **1 pt**: No Markdown artifacts (e.g., no \`\`\` code blocks, no LaTeX `$` signs).
*   **1 pt**: Google Docs Compatible (Unicode subscripts `CO₂`, correct Emojis 🅰️/🅱️).

## 2. Pedagogical Depth (4 Points)
*   **1 pt**: **Core Concept** is accurate, concise, and directly relevant to the specific question.
*   **1 pt**: **Step-by-Step Analysis** is logical, sequential, and leads clearly to the *intermediate results* and *final answer*.
*   **1 pt**: **Option Wise Explanation** explains *why* the wrong answers are wrong (refutes the misconception), not just stating they are incorrect.
*   **1 pt**: Language is appropriate for A/O-level students (clear, formal, academic but accessible).

## 3. Learning Value (3 Points)
*   **1 pt**: **Concept Gaps** identify specific cognitive pitfalls (e.g., "confusing mass with weight") rather than generic statements.
*   **1 pt**: **Flashcards** are testable (Front/Back format) and cover the *gap* identified.
*   **1 pt**: No "hallucinated" facts; content adheres to standard syllabus knowledge (9700/5090).

---

## QC Workflow
1.  **Read** the generated content.
2.  **Score** each question against the 10-point scale.
3.  **Result**:
    *   **Avg Score < 8**: REJECT & REGENERATE (Provide feedback on lost points).
    *   **Avg Score >= 8**: PASS.
